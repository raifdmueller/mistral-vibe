= Open Questions — Socratic Code-Theory Recovery
:toc: left

This is the Phase 1 handoff document. It lists only the `[OPEN]` leaves from
`QUESTION_TREE.adoc` — the facts that *cannot* be derived from code alone.
Route each group to the named role. Answers should be 1–3 sentences; they feed
Phase 2 (documentation synthesis), where each becomes a claim marked
`(team answer)`.

13 questions, grouped by Ask role.

== For the Product Owner

[cols="1,4",options="header"]
|===
| Q-ID | Question

| Q1.4
| Why does Mistral fund and open-source Mistral Vibe? Is the goal to drive
  Mistral API consumption, grow developer adoption, serve as a reference
  client, or something else? (Category: business-context)

| Q1.5
| What does success look like, and how is it measured — which adoption,
  retention, or conversion metrics is the product judged against?
  (Category: business-context)

| Q1.6
| When the needs of interactive developers, automation callers, and IDE
  integrators conflict, which user segment takes priority?
  (Category: stakeholder-context)

| Q2.10
| What was consciously decided to be *out of scope* — for example a web UI,
  multi-user collaboration, or first-class hosting of non-Mistral models?
  (Category: business-context)

| Q3.2.1
| What organizational or regulatory constraints apply beyond the Apache-2.0
  license — data-handling requirements, release-cadence commitments, support
  obligations? (Category: stakeholder-context)
|===

== For the Architect

[cols="1,4",options="header"]
|===
| Q-ID | Question

| Q3.4.1
| Why was the four-module layering (`core` / `cli` / `acp` / `setup`) chosen?
  Was sharing one engine across a CLI and an ACP front-end the driving
  reason? (Category: design-rationale)

| Q3.9.1.R
| Why was Textual chosen for the TUI rather than prompt_toolkit, a Rich-only
  interface, or a web UI? (Category: design-rationale)

| Q3.9.3.R
| Why does configuration use per-field merge strategies (replace, concat,
  union, shallow, conflict) instead of simple layer override? Which concrete
  config conflicts justified that complexity? (Category: design-rationale)

| Q3.9.6.R
| Why are sessions persisted as flat `meta.json` + `messages.jsonl` files
  rather than an embedded database such as SQLite — portability,
  human-readability, simplicity? (Category: design-rationale)

| Q3.10.1
| What are the prioritized, measurable quality targets — for example a
  startup-latency budget, a token-throughput goal, or a crash-rate ceiling?
  (Category: quality-goals)

| Q4.9
| Which ISO 25010 quality characteristic dominates when two conflict — e.g.
  does TUI responsiveness beat feature breadth, or does security gating beat
  convenience? (Category: quality-goals)

| Q5.6
| Is there a deprecation or debt-reduction roadmap — which subsystems are
  considered legacy or scheduled for replacement? (Category: future-direction)
|===
